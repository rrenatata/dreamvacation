Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the capital of The Netherlands, is an exquisite city filled with many canals and narrow houses (like the ones in San Francisco). It is a diverse city that has lots of culture and numerous things to do. From a varity of architecture to a diverse music scene, Amsterdam has everything!

One of the many appealing aspects of Amsterdam is its nightlife. There are many activities one can do when visiting the city. From riding bikes around the canals to taking a boat ride through them, one can enjoy the beauty of Amsterdam.
